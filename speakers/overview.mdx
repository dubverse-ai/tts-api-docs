# Speaker Overview

Dubverse offers a wide range of high-quality Text-to-Speech (TTS) voices powered by our advanced models:

1. neo.one <Icon icon="bolt" />: Our fast, efficient TTS model, designed for quick processing and resource optimization across a wide range of languages and use cases.

2. candy.two <Icon icon="star" />: Our flagship model focused on high-quality, natural-sounding speech synthesis, making it ideal for applications requiring premium voice output.

For a comprehensive list of available speakers with audio previews, please refer to the following pages:

- [Best Speakers](/speakers/best-speakers)
- [neo.one Speakers](/speakers/neo-one-speakers)
- [candy.two Speakers](/speakers/candy-two-speakers)

For more information on our TTS models and their capabilities, please refer to our [Models Overview](/models/overview) page.

To use these speakers in your projects or for any additional information, please contact our support team at friends@dubverse.ai.
